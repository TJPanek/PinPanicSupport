Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app, go to the upgrade screen, and tap Restore Purchases at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I back up my collection?

Go to Stats → Export Collection. This saves a JSON file to your device that you can store in iCloud Drive, AirDrop to another device, or keep as a backup.

How do I import a backup?

Go to Stats → Import Collection and select your previously exported JSON file. Imported pins are merged with your existing collection.

Why does the market value lookup show no results?

Market value is sourced from recent eBay sold listings. If no results appear, there may not be recent sales data for that pin. Try again later or search eBay directly.

How do I delete a pin?

Open the pin's detail view and scroll to the bottom, or swipe left on the pin in the collection grid and tap Delete.

What is the free pin limit?

The free version allows up to 100 pins in your collection and 100 pins on your wishlist. Upgrade to Pin Panic Pro or Pin Panic+ for unlimited pins.

Does Pin Panic work offline?

Yes. Your collection is stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only required for market value lookups and iCloud sync (Pin Panic+ subscribers).

Privacy Policy

No account required. Pin Panic works without creating an account. Your data belongs to you.

No third-party analytics. We do not use any third-party tracking or analytics SDKs.

Your collection stays on your device. All pin data is stored locally. Pin Panic+ subscribers sync via their private iCloud account — we never have access to your data.

Market value lookups. When you search for a pin's value, a query is sent to eBay to retrieve recent sold listing prices. No personal information is included in this request.

Camera & Photos. Camera and photo library access is used only to add images to your pin collection. Images are stored on your device and never uploaded to our servers.
